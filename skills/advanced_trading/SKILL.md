# Advanced Trading Skills - Learned 2026-02-19

## Professional Trading Framework

### Multi-Timeframe Analysis
1. Higher timeframe (Daily) = Trend direction
2. Middle timeframe (4H) = Entry zone
3. Lower timeframe (1H) = Precise entry

### Indicator Confluence Strategy
**5 Confirmation System:**
1. Trend Filter (EMA 50/200 alignment)
2. Momentum (RSI 14 - buy zone 40-60)
3. MACD crossover confirmation
4. Volume spike (>1.5x average)
5. Key level (S/R, Fibonacci)

### Pattern Recognition Library
1. **Bullish Patterns**: Double bottom, Inverse H&S, Bull flag, Ascending triangle
2. **Bearish Patterns**: Double top, H&S, Bear flag, Descending triangle
3. **Continuation**: Pennants, Triangles, Flags

### Entry Signal Checklist
- [ ] HTF trend confirmed
- [ ] Key level identified
- [ ] RSI in neutral zone (40-60 for trend continuation)
- [ ] MACD histogram expanding
- [ ] Volume above average
- [ ] Pattern complete
- [ ] Risk:Reward ≥ 2.5:1

### Exit Strategy
1. TP1: 1:2 R/R → Move SL to breakeven
2. TP2: 1:3 R/R → Close 50% of position
3. Trail remaining with ATR (2x)

### Market Regime Detection
- **Trending**: ADX > 25, EMAs aligned
- **Ranging**: ADX < 20, price between EMAs
- **Volatile**: ATR expanding, wide stops needed
- **Quiet**: ATR contracting, breakout coming
